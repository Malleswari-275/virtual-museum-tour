Dinosaur Exhibit

Travel back millions of years to the age of dinosaurs. Learn about these ancient reptiles, their habitat, and how they once ruled the Earth.

Tyrannosaurus Rex

Known as the "King of the Dinosaurs," the T. rex was a massive carnivorous predator with powerful jaws and sharp teeth.

Triceratops

A herbivorous dinosaur known for its three horns and large frill, used for defense and display.

Brachiosaurus

This giant long-necked dinosaur was a plant-eater that could reach the tops of tall trees in prehistoric forests.

Velociraptor

Small, fast, and intelligent, the Velociraptor used its claws and speed to hunt in packs.
